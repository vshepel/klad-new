FetoLife is a digital platform aimed to guide users throughout the pregnancy cycle. The company wanted to create a modern and supportive digital experience for its users, so we redesigned the website, application and branding by adjusting colours, type fonts and adding 3D models. Additionally, we designed and developed a set of digital tools, such as pregnancy calculators and weekly guides.

Comprehensively, we created web and brand animations, motion graphics, 3D models and implemented all the solutions with a custom-built CMS system and required integrations.
